Challah French Toast (GF)(DF)

Description

A delicious gluten free breakfast, great for saturday mornings

Ingredients

  • 1 gluten free challah
  • 2 eggs
  • Dash of oatmilk
  • 1 avocado
  • 1/4 lemon, squeezed
  • Olive oil
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Slice gluten free challah
  2. Crack eggs into bowl and add dash of oatmilk and sprinkle of salt
  3. Scramble eggs with a fork
  4. For each slice of gluten free challah
    • Lay flat in egg mixture for 10 seconds and turn over for 10 seconds
    • Pan fry for 1 minute on both sides (with olive oil)
  5. Smoosh up avocado and add lemon and sprinkle of salt to serve on challah french toast
